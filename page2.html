Interactions

BigBlue is partnered with the well-known The Ocean Conservancy. Together, they are planning multiple projects and hosting events together with the support of their members. As both are major organizations, their projects have been very successful and amazing for our environment.

Activities

According to (National Geographic, 2015), 5.25 Trillion tons of plastic is found in the ocean every year. Therefore, Bigblue is currently planning on expanding their research on an environment-friendly replacement for plastics. With this project, they hope to reduce the pollution and harm plastic has to the ocean. They also plan to continue their previous projects such as the coastal clean-ups, fund-raisers, and seminars for the ocean.
