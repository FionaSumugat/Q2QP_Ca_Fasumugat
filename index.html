Big Blue

Save Oceans, Save Lives

Organization Background

BigBlue is an organization aiming to save ocean life. Our projects aim to reduce water pollution and habitat destruction caused by trash and human activities. It was first founded in 2001 and has over a million members today.

What We Have Done So Far

So far, BigBlue has done countless projects for their cause. This includes hosting multiple coastal clean-up events, fundraisers for the ocean, and conducting research for alternatives to plastic to reduce waste. They have been raising awareness to the condition of our oceans today and are providing ways for other people to contribute to help our ocean. They have been featured in countless magazines and newspapers, even bringing themselves to the news.
