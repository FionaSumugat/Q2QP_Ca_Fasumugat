Who We Are

BigBlue is an environmentalist organization founded in 2010 by founder and current president, Fiona Sumugat. The organization was started by a small group of friends managing a small website and doing constant coastal cleanups. This eventually garnered the attention of the masses and it slowly grew to what it is today. As of now, BigBlue is one of the largest and most successful environmental organizations in the world.

Vision

BigBlue strives to create an environmentally-friendly world for everyone. We aim to create a world with less water pollution and less waste being scattered irresponsibly. BigBlue aims to train people from all ages, whether it be young or old, to help conserve our environment and raise awareness of its condition.

How to Help

There are countless ways to help our ocean! One of them is joining and becoming an active member of our organization! However, if it is difficult, then you can also help in your own way. One of these is segregating your own trash properly. This may seem like a small step, but it has a big impact on our environment. Encouraging your family and friends to do the same, can be tremendous help for our ocean. Another is to research and invite other people to partake in helping. This can include posters, blogs, websites, or even just a simple post on your social media. Raise awareness on the condition of our world and do as much as you can to help it.
